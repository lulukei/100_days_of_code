Recipe for a successful 100-day code challenge

Ingredients

  • Great interest for a topic (that can survive outside of the work context)
  • Humility towards your learning capacities
  • A bunch a equally motivated companions

Method

  1. Pick a topic you have great interest in
  2. Define the scope of your learning realistically (if you are a beginner, don't aim to reach the advanced level at the end of the 100 days)
  3. Find a great resource you can learn from (a MOOC, an vido tutorial series, or a book - books are great way to get your face off the screen!)
  4. Break it down into one hundred pieces (and evaluate if it is too much or too little for 100 days)
  5. Commit to 1 piece a day (more than one is fine - but the key idea here is endurance so make sure you don't use up all your mental ressources for the next day)
  6. Share daily with your learning companions for accountability
  7. Bonus: the cliffhanger technique. Skim through the challenge of the next day to get excited and sleep on it
